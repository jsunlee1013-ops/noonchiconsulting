PRIVACY POLICY

Noonchi Consulting LLC Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 16, 2025

Last Updated: January 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Noonchi Consulting LLC is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how Noonchi Consulting LLC collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you visit the website or engage consulting services, particularly in educational settings where student privacy is paramount.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Professional information (title, institution, role)
  • Communication preferences and interaction history

Educational Context Information:

  • Non-identifiable institutional data and performance metrics
  • Stakeholder feedback and assessment results
  • Implementation progress and outcome measurements

Website Information:

  • IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, referring websites
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Information

Information is collected and used to:

  • Provide consulting services and support
  • Communicate about projects and engagements
  • Improve services and methodologies
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Conduct research and analysis (anonymized data only)

4. Educational Data Protection

FERPA Compliance:

  • Noonchi Consulting LLC does not collect, store, or process personally identifiable student information
  • All educational data handled is aggregated and anonymized
  • Strict data separation is maintained between consulting insights and student records

Context Data Management:

  • MCP implementation systems use privacy-preserving context management
  • No persistent storage of identifiable student information without explicit consent
  • Automatic data purging according to institutional retention policies

5. Information Sharing

Noonchi Consulting LLC does not sell, trade, or rent personal information. Information may be shared only:

  • With explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With trusted service providers under strict confidentiality agreements
  • In anonymized form for research and improvement purposes

6. Data Security

Industry-standard security measures are implemented:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit (minimum AES-256)
  • Geographic data residency within the United States
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Limited access on a need-to-know basis

7. Data Retention

  • Personal information: Retained for the duration of our business relationship plus 7 years
  • Educational context data: Purged according to institutional policies
  • Website data: Retained for 2 years unless longer retention is required by law

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability
  • File complaints with appropriate regulatory authorities

9. Children's Privacy

Services are designed for educational professionals and administrators. Noonchi Consulting LLC does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or website notice.
